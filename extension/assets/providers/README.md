# Provider logos

Bundled brand icons used to identify the third-party AI providers ChatPanel can
connect to (nominative use). ChatPanel is not affiliated with or endorsed by
these companies; **all marks are the trademarks of their respective owners.**

SVG sources:
- gilbarbara/logos — CC0 1.0 (https://github.com/gilbarbara/logos)
- simple-icons — CC0 1.0, icon files (https://github.com/simple-icons/simple-icons)
- svgl — https://github.com/pheralb/svgl

`openrouter.svg` and `ollama.svg` are the same official marks recolored from
white to dark so they read on a light tile. Providers without a bundled logo
render a colored letter monogram instead.
